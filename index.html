Fluxbb-Social-Profile-Links

Adds Social links to the profile and viewtopic pages where users can add their usernames or user id's for, Care2, Delicious, Deviantart, Facebook, GitHub, Google+, Instagram, Pinterest, Stumbleupon, Tumblr, Twitter and YouTube.

Fluxbb-View-Own-Profile

You can see your profile like other people see your profile (not in edit mode).
